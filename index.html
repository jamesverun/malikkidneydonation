Frequently Asked Questions

What is kidney donation?

Kidney donation is the process of donating a healthy kidney to help a patient with kidney failure.

Is living donation safe?

Living kidney donation is carefully evaluated medically to ensure donor safety and health.

How can I contact support?

You can contact us through WhatsApp or email anytime for assistance.

Is communication confidential?

Yes, privacy and respectful communication are important priorities.
